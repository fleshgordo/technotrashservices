Green screen
Melina Reymondin & Arthur Groebli

Green Screen is a project aiming to recycle old screen to transform them in horticultural lamps. In fact, the recycling of screens is still very problematic, it is then very important to find a solution. Moreover, in a near future, soils could become quite hostile due to the climate change. This is why we would need horticultural lamps in order to guarantee the durability of enclosed agriculture. Green Screen offers lamps of various sizes depending on the screens and plants to grow. Moreover, this service offers different temperature and intensity of light in order to insure the growth process of plants.
